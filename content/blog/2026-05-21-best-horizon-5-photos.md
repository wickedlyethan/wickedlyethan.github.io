Merry *Forza Horizon*-mas to those who celebrate, and I really mean celebrate. Few games bring me as much joy as these open world zoom-em-ups and one the best parts is the photo mode. When you nail a composition your car becomes a sculptural object part of the landscape itself; conversely, it’s fun to show off a retro Dodge Challenger with a Hatsune Miku livery ramping off a sand dune. We can do both.

So far *Horizon* 6 is tickling my fancy but feels toned down and sometimes even stiff. The neon isn’t quite neon enough, the mud not thick and slinging, the sun shy and reserved. Maybe that’s Playground Games wanting to avoid the most egregious version of a *Nippon*-Epcot vibe, but we’ll see if that changes as we get further into it and the seasons change.

Inspired by nathan’s post I wanted to curate some of my favorite shots from *Horizon* 5, which I think is the most varied and colorful setting the game has had. Surely I'll do another one for 6; wrapping up a play session by sending shots to the Discord is part of the fun.
