iPhone 17 with Halide Mark III

Yeah I'm pretty in love with Halide. The new version of the app has totally revitalized phone photography for me – I had my Kodak Snapic A1 with me for most of these shots, but I won't see those results for almost a month, so shooting with the new film emulations became addictive. The halation and grain in particular are marvelous, and the iPhone 17's camera is so sharp that softening it is absolutely the right move. Process Zero is fun but correctly not very expressive; "Nova" and "Zephyr" are very expressive and opinionated.
