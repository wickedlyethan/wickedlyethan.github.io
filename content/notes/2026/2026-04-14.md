Discord is an obnoxious, currently necessary piece of software in my life and I think it's a great example of every piece of software getting worse and never nailing the basics.

On macOS there is a long-running bug where, when the machine comes out of sleep, Discord farts a clipped, maximum-volume klaxon even if you've already seen those notifications elsewhere. You are getting shouted at "HEY GET THE F- oh nevermind" every single time. Meanwhile the mobile app doesn't respect Do Not Disturb settings, so Discord notifications will bother you no matter what.

These bugs by themselves are obnoxious but they've also been around a long time, so what exactly are folks working on over there, or anywhere? Every software I use made by a large company seems to be getting shittier – macOS itself included.
