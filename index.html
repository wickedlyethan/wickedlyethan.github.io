Ethan Thibault

Producer and Writer

Hey! I'm Ethan and I'm an aspiring writer and sometimes game-maker. I love all things tech and talking about media studies while walking in the woods.

I most recently was a Digital Marketing Technologist for higher education at Carnegie Dartlet. Before that I worked at Curriculum Associates, Viewpoint Creative, and FableVision.

I'm a creative problem-solver and am passionate about helping everyone on a team do their best work through information architecture, down-to-earth processes, great tools, and candid communication.

I'm very lucky to get to work on awesome projects when I can. Here are some favorites:

  • ROCK & ROLL - A musical TTRPG about making the band of your dreams with your friends and experiencing all the highs and lows of life on-stage and off. Kickstarted as part of Zine Quest 2019.
  • Embers Falling - A small TTRPG about power and duty for a stranded mech crew made for Emotional Mecha Jam.
  • JetHack - First-person jetpacking to destroy mega-corporate advertisements made for Global Game Jam 2018
  • Atleta - A hand-drawn sailing interactive fiction adventure made for Global Game Jam 2016
